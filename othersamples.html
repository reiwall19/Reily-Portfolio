Task Talent

Description: have you ever tried using your calendar to track due dates, important meetings, appointments? Well Task Talent is a web app that allows YOU to create YOUR OWN tasks, edit them and delete them. You can give them due dates, personalize your app as you see fit with colours and layouts and create as many as you'd like, all to track your important events.

Tech Stack: React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Recipe Royale

Description: This app is great for all food enthusiasts who claim to say their recipe is superior. You can input your own recipe into the application, and allows other foodies to see and try it for themselves. The app allows other chefs to review and rate your recipe, finally marking an end with all the claims of having the best recipe.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, API Integration
